Myriad Evo — Partner & Client Handbook

A living web-doc that explains our onboarding, idea flow, payments, and fairness rules.

Contents

1. Mission & Pillars

At Myriad Evo, our mission is to produce more sales, more engagement, and more visibility for every client we serve.

Pillars

  • Innovation — continuously finding new ways to grow.
  • Scalability — building solutions that adapt and expand.
  • Impact — delivering measurable results for clients.

2. Partner Onboarding

Target group: students and non-experienced talents. Entry requirement: learn fast, be creative, and align with our mission.

First 6 months — task-based payment only. After review → possible permanent contract (base salary + revenue sharing).

3. Two Models of Work

Existing Clients (Continuous Improvement)

We already delivered their project. Partners propose improvements; best implementations are pitched. Categories: Basic / Gold / Premium.

New Clients (Fresh Projects)

Client needs are analyzed and broken into features. Features are published internally and partners implement the best solutions.

4. Idea & Feature Workflow

Step A — Submission

Idea submitted (short pitch + expected impact). Implementations encouraged before submission.

Step B — Review

Reviewed for viability & alignment. Categorized: Basic / Gold / Premium.

Step C — Implementation

Idea becomes public internally. Any partner can implement. Best implementation chosen.

Step D — Client Pitch

Agency pitches to client. If accepted, feature sold. If rejected, contributor still gets recognition.

Step E — Payment

Basic paid from maintenance. Gold/Premium paid from client feature fee. Default split: Idea 30% / Execution 70% (100% if same person).

5. Partner Growth Path

Evaluation after 6 months based on contributions, code quality, ideas, and client impact. Top performers offered permanent contracts and work on independent projects.

6. Client Communication

We present ourselves as an innovation partner — "We continuously analyze and propose improvements. You only pay for approved features." This is our USP.

7. Operations & Tracking

Start simple: Notion / Airtable / Trello / GitHub Issues. Track submissions, implementations, payments, and partner stats. Later, build an internal platform with AI for review + automation.

8. Conflict Resolution & Fairness

8.1 Categorization Disputes

Partners may appeal once. Lead examiner (or board/AI) reviews. Final decision documented and binding.

8.2 Execution Disputes

Competing implementations reviewed for quality and scalability. Chosen solution published with explanation. Non-chosen contributors may get recognition credits.

8.3 Idea Ownership

Ideas become agency property on submission; authors always receive credit + 30% share if implemented.

8.4 Transparency & Records

All steps are logged. Each partner can see their contribution history.

8.5 Code of Collaboration

Be respectful. No sabotage or personal attacks. Violations may lead to removal.
